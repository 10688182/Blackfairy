About Us

Our Vision

The vision of Blackfairygh Beauty Services is to redefine beauty and self-care by providing an unparalleled salon experience that inspires confidence and self-expression. We strive to be a trusted and innovative leader in the beauty industry, setting new standards for excellence, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is to create a welcoming space where clients feel valued, pampered, and empowered to embrace their unique beauty.

Founder

“My name is Pamela Cleo, and I am the founder of Blackfairygh Beauty Services. Growing up, I was always fascinated by the transformative power of beauty—not just as a means of enhancing one’s appearance, but as a way to boost confidence and celebrate individuality. As a woman of color, I often struggled to find beauty products and services that truly catered to my unique needs, and that’s what inspired me to create Blackfairygh Beauty Services.”

— Pamela Cleo
